Events

Team Kshamta, the Social Responsibility Forum of SVKM's NMIMS, organized and executed many events successfully. The following is an overall description of events that took place in the years 2023-2024.

Swasthya

Kshamta organised its successful educational and healthcare venture in the form of "Swasthya”. The event featured health assessments for around 217 students from Grades 6 to 8, including BMI measurements, blood group testing, and consultations with various doctors. Donation boxes were set up before the event to collect items from teachers and students. The event concluded with closing remarks, expressions of gratitude to participants and volunteers , and the doctors' felicitation. Overall, "Swasthya" was a resounding success, fostering connections and positively impacting the young minds in attendance.

Aashayein

Kshamta, organised an event titled "Aashayein" to support children undergoing cancer treatment. The event included only the core team to minimise overcrowding and protect vulnerable patients. The participants from team Kshamta gained insight into the challenges faced by the children and their families. The event featured activities designed to engage and uplift the children. Overall, "Aashayein" succeeded in providing moments of joy and support for the children, emphasising the importance of community engagement and compassion in difficult times.

Beach Cleanup

Kshamta organised a Beach Clean Up Drive in collaboration with PPA India that attracted numerous participants, including Kshamta members, college volunteers and environmental enthusiasts. The participants were provided with gloves and garbage bags. Following the beach cleanup, volunteers had the option to clean nearby mangroves, where they learned to collect garbage without harming the trees. The event concluded with participants successfully collecting two tonnes of garbage. The initiative not only cleaned the beach but also educated volunteers about the impacts of plastic waste and pollution, marking the event as a significant success.

Aarogya

Kshamta, held its flagship event ‘Aarogya’ to provide comprehensive healthcare services to individuals. The event included participants and students from the NGOs. Students from various NGOs arrived for this event and were provided with checkups from doctors of different specialities. The team kept the children engaged with games and interactive sessions.The event concluded with the doctors being honoured who checked a total number of 800 people that involved active participation from committee members, faculty, and medical professionals, marking a heartwarming end to a year focused on societal betterment.

Vriksharopan

Kshamta, in partnership with PPA, is powered by Rotary Club, and supported by MNCDF. organised "Vriksharopan," a tree plantation drive. The began with an educational session on the benefits of tree planting, followed by each department of Kshamta participating in rounds to plant and nurture 100+ saplings. This impactful event emphasised the importance of tree conservation in the face of urban development and highlighted the essential role of trees in sustaining life

Vidya

Kshamta, organised its first career counselling event, "Vidya,". The event aimed to guide students on various career paths, with 70 students attending and 18 stalls representing fields in Science, Commerce, and Humanities. The event began with a speaker session on navigating career choices and awareness about the law. Afterwards, students visited stalls where Kshamta members offered guidance on careers across different fields, including details on eligibility, entrance exams, and career prospects. Each student received educational resources as well as a folder and pen. The event concluded with the students feeling inspired and informed about their career choices.

Breast Cancer Awareness

Kshamta hosted a Breast Cancer Awareness session that aimed to educate attendees on symptoms and self-examination. Key participants included TATA Trust members and oncologist Dr. Amar Deshpande. The team of TATA Trust led an icebreaker, and a film underscored the importance of recognizing lumps. Dr. Deshpande discussed survival rates, risk factors, and early detection, followed by a Q&A on genetics and symptoms. Brochures on self-examination were available in multiple languages. The event concluded with gratitude to Dr. Deshpande and the TATA Trust, successfully promoting awareness and reducing stigma around breast cancer.

Kartavya

Kshamta successfully conducted ‘Kartavya’ with the aim of bringing warmth and joy to the elders as well as the orphans of the society. Through this initiative, the team visited orphanages and old-age homes, organising engaging activities and interactive sessions to create unforgettable memories. Kartavya embodies the mission of Kshamta of making a positive impact in the society.

Karuna

Kshamta successfully conducted its blood donation drive ‘Karuna’. This initiative aimed to address the critical issue of blood shortages and promote volunteerism within the community. The event witnessed a notable surge in participation during the lunch hour, ultimately exceeding the target of 100 donors. The day concluded with a celebration in recognition of this collective effort.

Food Donation Drive

The success of the Food Donation Drive lies in the provision of smile-inducing, wholesome and nutritious meals to economically weaker sections of society.

Udaan

Udaan is a vibrant sports day event organized by Kshamta SRF, bringing together underprivileged children for a day filled with fun, games, and camaraderie. This joyful celebration creates a platform for kids to showcase their talents, embrace teamwork, and experience the spirit of sportsmanship. Through Udaan, Kshamta SRF not only spreads smiles but also instills confidence and a sense of belonging, empowering these young participants to dream big and aim high
